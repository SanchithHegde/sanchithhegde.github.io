Sowing the Seeds of My Digital Garden

Jan 4 2021

With Linux being my primary OS for development and daily use, I come across a lot of new things (Linux-specific and otherwise) on the daily, which help improve my development experience and sometimes increase productivity. This blog digital garden will hopefully serve as a index for those findings, and an excuse for me to properly research and document them for myself and others.

The inspiration for me to use the term “digital garden” should be primarily attributed to this post by Joel Hooks, which in turn led me to another interesting piece, building a digital garden by Tom Critchlow.
